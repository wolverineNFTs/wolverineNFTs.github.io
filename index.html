So, what exactly are NFT's?

An NFT, or Non-Fungible Token, is a digital asset that represents real-world objects like art, music, and videos.

NFTs from Wolverine Collectibles are artistic digital relics that represent the unforgettable Michigan Experience. Remember your first day in the dorms? Your first bite of a slice from Joe's? The deafening roar of the Big House when we beat Ohio State? That deep breath you took right after your last exam? We could go on, but the memories are truly endless.

Wolverine Collectibles immortalizes these amazing times at Michigan. Get your collectible and celebrate the Michigan Experience. You can purchase one on an NFT marketplace.
