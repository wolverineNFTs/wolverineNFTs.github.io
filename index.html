What are NFT's?

An NFT is... culture. Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) are modern art. Think of the Mona Lisa, The Starry Night, Girl with a Pearl Earring. What makes these pieces of art valuable? The visceral culture exuding out of each work. The sweat and tears put into them, and what each work represents. That's why we created Wolverine NFTs -- because we care about raw, Michigan culture.
